We are Research-driven
What does that mean?

We Value Quality

The Centre for Integrated Development Research and Action (CIDRA) is a dynamic, Rwanda-based organization committed to driving sustainable development through evidence-based solutions. We specialize in consulting, training, research, community engagement, youth empowerment, and women’s support, delivering programs that bridge the gap between policy, practice, and impact. Our mission is to turn data-driven insights into action that transforms lives and strengthens communities across Africa.

  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Inclusivity
  • Excellence

CIDRA was founded with a bold vision: to create an institution where research meets action for lasting change. Recognizing that development challenges are often complex and interconnected, we bring together experts, communities, and policymakers to design practical solutions grounded in evidence. From local initiatives to regional projects, CIDRA stands as a catalyst for inclusive growth, innovation, and empowerment.
